Restaurant USSD

Ordering and information system via mobile USSD codes

Overview

Restaurant USSD is a mobile application that allows users to browse menus, place orders, and access restaurant information using USSD codes. It is designed for accessibility, allowing people without smartphones or internet access to interact with restaurants easily.

Motivation Behind Restaurant USSD

Not everyone has access to food delivery apps, especially in regions with limited internet. The motivation was to make ordering food simple, inclusive, and fast. Users can access menus, check availability, and place orders directly from basic mobile phones.
