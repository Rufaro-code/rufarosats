DrowzyKnight

Driver safety and alertness optimization app

Overview

DrowzyKnight is a safety-focused application designed to monitor drivers’ alertness and provide real-time feedback to prevent accidents caused by drowsiness. It combines facial recognition, eye-tracking, and AI algorithms to detect signs of fatigue and issue timely warnings. The platform can also generate reports for fleet managers to analyze driver behavior and enhance overall safety.

Motivation Behind DrowzyKnight

While working with transport and logistics teams, I noticed a recurring problem: driver fatigue was a major cause of accidents. Many drivers struggled with maintaining alertness on long trips, and existing solutions were either too expensive or intrusive. I wanted to create an affordable, reliable solution that could monitor alertness without being disruptive. DrowzyKnight was born to save lives, improve driver productivity, and give fleet managers actionable insights.

Key Features

  • 👀 Real-time eye-tracking to detect drowsiness
  • 🔔 Audible and visual alerts when fatigue is detected
  • 📊 Daily and weekly driver reports for analysis
  • 🧠 AI-powered behavior prediction to anticipate fatigue
  • 📱 Mobile-friendly interface for easy monitoring

Tech Stack

  • ⚡ Python and OpenCV for real-time video processing
  • 🧠 TensorFlow for fatigue detection and predictive analytics
  • 📱 React Native for mobile dashboard and alerts
  • 💾 SQLite and cloud storage for logs and reporting

Challenges & Solutions

One of the main challenges was achieving accurate real-time detection without heavy computational requirements. Initially, the system had latency issues, but by optimizing the neural network and using lightweight models, I improved performance. Another challenge was designing alerts that were noticeable but not distracting. I iterated through multiple sound tones and visual indicators to find a balance that ensured safety.

Lessons Learned

This project reinforced my understanding of AI in real-world applications. I gained deep insights into computer vision, neural networks, and system optimization. I also learned the importance of human-centered design: even the best AI system fails if users find it intrusive or hard to use.

Future Improvements

Future plans include integrating wearable devices for biometric monitoring, adding speech-based alerts, and expanding predictive analytics to forecast fatigue trends over long-term data. The goal is to make DrowzyKnight a comprehensive safety suite for drivers everywhere.
