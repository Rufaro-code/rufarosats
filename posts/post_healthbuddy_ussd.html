HealthBuddy USSD

Mobile health tips and guidance via USSD

Overview

HealthBuddy USSD is a mobile-based application designed to provide users with quick health tips, guidance, and information without needing internet connectivity. The app uses USSD codes, making it accessible to anyone with a basic phone.

Motivation Behind HealthBuddy USSD

Many people in remote areas lack reliable internet access yet need essential health information. The motivation for creating HealthBuddy USSD was to bridge this gap and deliver important health guidance directly to users’ mobile phones, empowering them to take charge of their health without barriers.

Key Features

  • 📞 USSD-based interface for basic phones
  • 💊 Health tips across multiple categories (nutrition, exercise, hygiene)
  • 🩺 Symptom checker with simple guidance
  • 📋 Reminders for vaccinations and medical check-ups
  • 🌍 Accessible in low-internet or offline conditions

Tech Stack

  • 🟢 Node.js backend to process USSD requests
  • 📡 USSD Gateway integration
  • 🗄️ Database for storing tips, reminders, and logs
  • 💻 Lightweight CLI interface for admin management

Challenges & Solutions

Designing a simple but effective USSD menu that covers diverse health topics was challenging. I created hierarchical menus with intuitive navigation and implemented caching for faster response times. Testing with different mobile devices ensured compatibility.

Lessons Learned

This project improved my understanding of USSD technology, low-bandwidth application design, and inclusive technology for populations with limited connectivity. It emphasized user-centered design even with minimalistic interfaces.

Future Improvements

Future plans include expanding content with local language support, integrating voice-based tips for illiterate users, and partnering with health institutions to provide verified information directly through the app.
